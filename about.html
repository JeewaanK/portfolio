How I Think

Investment approach

In each report, the setup was the same: extract the specific number the market is implying, find primary data that disputes it, and build a case that is falsifiable.

On NuScale, a reverse DCF extracted the market-implied pipeline conversion probability: 45.2%. Stage-gate close rates put the correct estimate at 65%. That 20-point gap is the thesis. On ULTA, Placer.ai foot traffic data showed ULTA gaining share while specialty retail declined, months before it appeared in same-store sales. The saturation narrative the market was pricing had already been contradicted by observable data.
